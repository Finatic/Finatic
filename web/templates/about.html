About Finatic

Finatic is the goto platform for portfolio and investment analytics. Our quantitative tools help you make informed data-driven investment decisions.

Our first product Portfolio Analyzer gives a complete analytical report on your Portfolio's backtested Performance, Risk Structure, Monte Carlo forecasts and much more:

  • Model and analyze the performance of different portfolios based on our rich set of historical data
  • Simulate and optimize portfolios based on historical returns
  • Identify and test the risk factor exposures of your portfolio
  • Test tactical asset allocations models that aim to offer better risk adjusted performance
  • Generate reports and export data in PDF, Excel, and CSV formats

Our Products
